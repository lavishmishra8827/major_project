Introduction

Students are now-a-days increasingly turning to online texts to supplement classroom material. However, these texts don't come with the review questions which are crucial in reinforcing one's concept and crafting them themselves can be extremely time consuming for both teachers as well student. By automatically generating questions, the project will be able to make learning and teaching process efficient for students and teachers. In long run, this software would represent a step towards automatically creating mini-courses from any online text.

How to use?

  • Enter the text.

    You can enter the passage by clicking on the "Enter the text" button, and then write the passage in the blank spacce shown in the screen.

  • Upload PDF file.

    You may upload the pdf file of the document by browsing your computer. Just click on the "Upload the pdf" button.

  • Generate Questions.

    After entering the text or uploading the pdf file, click on "Generate Questions" button and then the quetsions will be generated on the screen.
